How does this work?

We take your text and submit it to an AI model which makes the required adjustments that you specify. If you leave all of the sliders where they are your text will be unchanged. If you drag them to the right they will adjust accordingly. The further right you go the more it will adjust.

Bear in mind that formal language tends to be shorter than emotive language, so don't be surpised if your text gets shortened when you increase formality.
